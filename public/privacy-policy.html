HomeCrafter — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 21, 2026

Information We Collect

When you contact HomeCrafter or request a quote, we may collect your name, phone number, email address, and property address to provide our fencing and railing services.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide estimates
  • Schedule and complete fencing projects
  • Send appointment reminders and project updates via SMS or email
  • Follow up on completed work

SMS/Text Messaging

If you opt in to receive text messages by checking the SMS consent box on our form, you agree to receive transactional text messages from HomeCrafter related to your inquiry or project, including estimates, scheduling updates, and follow-ups.

  • Consent is optional. You are not required to opt in to SMS to submit a form, receive services, or make a purchase.
  • Message frequency varies based on your project status and communications.
  • Message and data rates may apply depending on your wireless carrier and plan.
  • Reply STOP to any message to opt out at any time.
  • Reply HELP for assistance.
  • We will not share your phone number with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • Supported carriers include AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon, and all major US carriers.

For questions about SMS messaging, contact us at trent@homecrafter.ai or reply HELP to any message.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share information with service providers who assist in our operations (e.g., scheduling, communications) under strict confidentiality agreements.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.
