Frequently Asked Questions
Do credits expire?
No. Your lead credits never expire. Use them whenever you're ready — there's no rush and no deadline.
Which bundle is right for me?
Choose the bundle that matches your trade from the dropdown above. If you're just getting started, a 10-pack is a great way to test the waters. The 25-pack offers the best balance of savings and commitment, while the 50-pack maximizes your discount at 20% off.
How quickly do I receive leads?
Leads are delivered as they come in within your service area. The busier your area, the faster you'll receive them.
What info do I get with each lead?
Every lead includes the homeowner's full name, address, phone number, email, property details, and project notes — everything you need to reach out and win the job.
How many contractors get each lead?
Each lead is shared with a maximum of 3 contractors — not 10+ like other platforms. Fewer competitors means a higher close rate for you.
