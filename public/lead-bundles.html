Frequently Asked Questions
Do credits expire?
No. Your lead credits never expire. Use them whenever you're ready — there's no rush and no deadline.
Can I use credits across different services?
Yes! Bundle credits work across all HomeCrafter services — FenceCrafter, RoofCrafter, WindowCrafter, SidingCrafter, and PaintCrafter.
How quickly do I receive leads?
Leads are delivered as they come in within your service area. The busier your area, the faster you'll receive them.
What info do I get with each lead?
Every lead includes the homeowner's full name, address, phone number, email, property details, and project notes — everything you need to reach out and win the job.
What's the difference between bundles and subscriptions?
Bundles let you buy a set number of lead credits up front — great for testing or seasonal work. Subscriptions give you unlimited leads in your zip codes for a flat monthly rate, which is more cost-effective at higher volumes.
