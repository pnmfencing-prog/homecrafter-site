Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How does the AI know about my business?
During onboarding, you provide details about your services, pricing, service area, and availability. We use this to train your assistant so it responds accurately — just like you would.
Will leads know they're talking to an AI?
Your assistant introduces itself as a representative of your company. The communication is warm, professional, and natural. Most leads won't notice a difference — they'll just appreciate the fast response.
Can I customize what the assistant says?
Absolutely. During setup we tailor tone, messaging, and responses to match your brand. You can request changes at any time, and the Professional plan includes ongoing optimization.
What if a lead has a complex question?
Your assistant handles the vast majority of inquiries. For anything outside its scope, it gracefully hands off to you with full context — so you step in informed, not blind.
Is there a contract or commitment?
Monthly plans have no commitment — cancel anytime. Annual plans lock in a lower rate with onboarding fees waived, billed once per year. Either way, no hidden fees.
How quickly can I get started?
Most assistants are live within 3–4 business days after you complete the onboarding form. You'll get a test interaction to review before anything goes live.
